Especialista en Bases de Datos Sr. de GCR

Titulo Especialista en Bases de Datos Sr. de GCR
Empresa Amazon
Localización Bellevue, WA, USA
Salario $59,000 - $143,000

Amazon's Selling Partner Risk organization is seeking an exceptional Business Analyst to drive analysis and insights to help our teams make meaningful business decisions. As a Business Analyst, you will focus on solving real world problems and build business cases to enable the organization to scale and invent on behalf of its customers. A key component of the role is to identify improvement opportunities by monitoring existing metrics, analyzing data, and partnering with many internal teams. In addition, you will design and develop automated reporting solutions to enable stakeholders to manage the business and make effective decisions. Lastly, you will enable effective decision making by retrieving and aggregating data from multiple sources and compiling it into a digestible and actionable format. The ideal candidate will be expected to influence and work with other leaders within the Selling Partner Risk organization.
